:Nutshells are a great tool for "expandable explanations." This is an example of a :hidden nutshell. When you click it, the hidden heading below will reveal itself here. Notice the "#" at the beginning of the link destination (which indicates a link to a heading on the current page). Notice that the displayed text of the link begins with ":". That's how Nutshell knows to transform it.

:x Hidden Heading Name

This will be invisible until it is revealed. The heading text must begin with ":x" to hide itself.
