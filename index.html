:Nutshells are a great tool for "expandable explanations." You can use them by simply adding :a few lines to your code.

This is an example of a :hidden nutshell for your own content. When you click it, the hidden heading below will reveal itself here. It uses the "#" symbol at the beginning of the link destination (which indicates a link to a heading on the current page). The displayed text of the link must begin with ":" as that's how Nutshell knows to transform it.

:x Hidden Heading Name

This will be invisible until it is revealed. The heading text must begin with ":x" to hide itself.
